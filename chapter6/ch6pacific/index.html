Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a very special lodging experience on the California North Coast with stunning views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay at the Pacific Trails Resort includes complimentary drinks, private dining with a chef, a yurt, and a quick-to-order breakfast every morning of your stay.

  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool and whirlpool
  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours to discover secret views
  • Enjoy special dining nights with live music and entertainment
  • Savor nightly fine dining with a breathtaking ocean view
