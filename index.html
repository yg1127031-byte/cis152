Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a very special lodging experience on the California North Coast with stunning views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay includes complimentary drinks, dining with a private chef, and a yurt along with a made-to-order breakfast every morning.

  • Relax in our high-class lodge while enjoying complimentary desserts, handcrafted wine, and beverages.
  • Take a refreshing walk by our natural hot springs lake or enjoy an evening spa session.
  • Unwind at our dining nights with live music and entertainment.
  • Hike our secret trails with guided tours and breathtaking coastal views.
