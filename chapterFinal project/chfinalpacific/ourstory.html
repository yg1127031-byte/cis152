Our Story

Where nature and comfort meet on California’s breathtaking coast.

Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort

Nestled along the rugged California coastline, Pacific Trails Resort was founded with a single vision—to create a retreat where guests can reconnect with nature, find peace in the rhythm of the ocean, and experience the simple beauty of outdoor living.

As the resort’s owner and lead designer, I wanted to build a place that blends comfort and sustainability. Our team shares a passion for protecting the environment while offering authentic outdoor adventures, including guided hikes, kayaking excursions, and bird-watching tours.

Whether you’re relaxing in one of our handcrafted yurts or exploring the surrounding trails, our goal is to provide an unforgettable experience that nurtures your body, mind, and spirit. Welcome to Pacific Trails—where your journey begins.
