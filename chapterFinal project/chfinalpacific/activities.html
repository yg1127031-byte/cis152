Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 6 miles of hiking trails, and it’s adjacent to the state park. Go on your own or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

Anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, but we also offer guided trips at sunrise several times a week.
