Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California north coast with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay includes private yurts with decks overlooking the ocean, activities such as kayaking, hiking, and bird watching, and delicious meals prepared with local organic ingredients.

  • Private yurts with decks overlooking the ocean
  • Activities including hiking, kayaking, and bird watching
  • Meals prepared with local organic ingredients
  • Guided tours of local attractions
