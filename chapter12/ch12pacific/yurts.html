The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

A yurt is a circular tent with a wooden frame covered in canvas. Ours are fully furnished with luxury amenities.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt has a queen-size bed with luxury linens, a gas fireplace, and a private deck.

What should I bring?

Bring a sense of adventure and a love for the outdoors. Everything else is provided.
