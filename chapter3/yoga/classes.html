Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
This is intended for beginners and anyone who is wishing for a grounded foundation into there practice of yoga, this class is only 60 minutes of poses and a slow movement focuson asana (a proper aligment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and a guided meditation to foster your mind and body connevtion.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although this is designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are very welcome to sample this class for 60 minutes that focuses on breathing-synchronized movements — you will inhale and exhale as you flow through energetically yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minutes class features a very slow movemnet and long poses that are very supported by a chair and wall. This is a calming, restorative experience for any suitable students of any level of experience. The practice can be perfect help to rehabilitate any type of injury.
