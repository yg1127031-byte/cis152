What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are our permanent structures four our feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, awooden floor, and with a very wunderful roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts finished?
Each yurt is finished with very white queen-size bed with a down uilt and a gas-fired stove. your luxury camping experience includes electricity facilities that are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guest have brought there very own comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for the camping weather with light layers of clothing. It's also very helpful to bring a flashlight and a high sense of adventure
