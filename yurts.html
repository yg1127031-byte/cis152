The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures set four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a beautiful roof dome that can be opened for stargazing.
How are the yurts finished?
Each yurt features a queen-size bed with a down quilt and a gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and modern facilities located in the lodge.
